Kamado Restoration

A slide show story of a Kamado that went from free to fantastic. A Nextdoor.com neighbor gave away his old Kamado grill. I spent a few bucks making it look nice.

Day 1: Not bad for free! Obviously, some rust issues and the paint is pretty faded. No handle. The nest casters are frozen up.

The rust from the metal top seemed to have leaked into the paint on the lid. Safe to assume it was left out in the rain many times

There's no indication of any brand. Later it becomes clear that there must of been something on the front of the lid that was sanded down. Found a Big Green Egg nest on Craigslist for 40 bucks!
