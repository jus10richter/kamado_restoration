Kamado Restoration Project

A slide show story of a Kamado that went from free to fantastic

Nextdoor.com neighbor gave away his old Kamado grill. I was extrememly excited, but I did want to at least spend a few bucks making it look nice. Having almost no knowledge of Kamado grills or their history, I decided to spend a great deal of time researching. A couple of months later here is the result.
